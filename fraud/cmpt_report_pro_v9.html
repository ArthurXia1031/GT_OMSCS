How to read. The Health Score (0–100) starts at 100, then deducts by deviation state — Attack −50 · Bleed −35 · Anomaly −25 · Early‑signal −15 — and −5 for each consecutive day AFPR stays >1σ off its 30‑day mean (≥90 healthy · 70–89 watch · <70 act). The status dot is green when today's AFPR sits inside its 30‑day ±2σ control band, red when it breaks out (beyond 2σ = rule‑level action). AFPR = total accounts ÷ fraud accounts — higher means more over‑blocking.
